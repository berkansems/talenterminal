How it works?

If your company needs a developer, all you need is filling this form. Our team will analyse your request and search for best candidates who fit your criteria using AI technology.

Company Applies a Job Position

Companies fill the form and specify the Qualifications, Requirements and Technologies needed for the job position.

We Review Company Request

Our team member search among hundreds of CVs to find the best employees compatible with your requisitions. All the survivors undergo the screening call (soft skills and language proficiency check)

Hard Skills Check

The list of remaining heroes send to the company to check candidates technical skills by face to face interview.

The final step, Contract!

Our concept is "No Cure, No Pay" we just ask for money if we could find right person to you.
